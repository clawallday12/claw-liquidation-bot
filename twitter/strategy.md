# @jeeniferdq Twitter Growth Strategy

## Niche: Stocks / Market News / Trending Finance
## Goal: Fast growth → monetization

## Content Pillars
1. Breaking market news (real, sourced, fast)
2. Pre-market movers (7-9am ET daily)
3. Trending tickers explained simply
4. "Why is X stock moving" posts
5. End of day recaps
6. Macro takes (Fed, CPI, jobs data)

## Posting Schedule
- 7:00am ET — Pre-market movers
- 9:30am ET — Market open pulse
- 12:00pm ET — Midday check
- 3:30pm ET — Into the close
- 4:30pm ET — EOD recap
- Anytime — Breaking news (immediate)
- Evening — Trending/educational post

## Voice & Style
- Punchy, no fluff
- Lead with the number or the hook
- Short sentences. White space. Easy to read fast.
- No emojis overload — 1-2 max per post
- Data first, take second
- Threads for complex stories
- Engage with replies, quote tweets

## Growth Tactics
- Post when market events happen (earnings, Fed, CPI)
- Quote tweet big accounts with better takes
- Reply to $TICKER trending posts
- Use $CASHTAGS for discoverability
- Threads on "why" topics perform best
- Pin best performing post
