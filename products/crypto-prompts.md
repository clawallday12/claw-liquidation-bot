### Prompt 1 — Altcoin Breakout Scanner
> "You are a crypto technical analyst. Given the following list of altcoins and their 24h price/volume data, identify the top 3 candidates showing breakout setups (volume spike >2x average, price crossing 20-day high, RSI 50–70). Rank by conviction and explain entry, stop-loss, and target for each."

---

### Prompt 2 — BTC Dominance Trade Thesis
> "Analyze the current Bitcoin dominance trend and tell me: (1) Is BTC.D rising or falling? (2) What does this imply for altcoin season timing? (3) Which 3 altcoin sectors (L1s, DeFi, AI tokens, etc.) are best positioned to outperform BTC if dominance drops below [X]%? Give me a trade thesis in 200 words."

---

### Prompt 3 — On-Chain Whale Alert Interpreter
> "Here is a list of recent large on-chain transactions: [paste wallet data]. Identify patterns that suggest accumulation, distribution, or exchange inflows/outflows. Summarize what smart money appears to be doing and what retail traders should watch in the next 48–72 hours."

---

### Prompt 4 — Funding Rate Arbitrage Finder
> "Given the following perpetual futures funding rates across exchanges [paste data], identify the top opportunities for funding rate arbitrage or fade trades. For each opportunity, explain the risk, the expected annualized yield, and whether it's more suitable for spot holders or derivatives traders."

---

### Prompt 5 — News Sentiment → Price Impact Predictor
> "Read the following crypto news headlines: [paste 5–10 headlines]. For each headline, score sentiment (bullish/bearish/neutral) and estimate the likely short-term price impact (0–4h, 4–24h) on the most directly affected tokens. Flag any that could trigger liquidation cascades."

---

### Prompt 6 — Portfolio Stress Test
> "My crypto portfolio is: [list assets + %]. Stress test this portfolio against 3 scenarios: (1) BTC drops 30% in 7 days, (2) a major stablecoin loses its peg, (3) a top-5 exchange gets hacked. For each scenario, estimate my drawdown, identify the weakest position, and suggest one hedge per scenario."

---

### Prompt 7 — Tokenomics Red Flag Detector
> "Analyze the tokenomics of [project name / paste whitepaper excerpt]: total supply, circulating supply, unlock schedule, team allocation %, and vesting cliff dates. Flag any red flags that suggest incoming sell pressure. Give me a 'rug risk' score from 1–10 with reasoning."

---

### Prompt 8 — DeFi Yield Optimizer
> "I have [X amount] in USDC/ETH/BTC to put to work in DeFi. Compare the top 5 yield opportunities right now across lending protocols, liquidity pools, and liquid staking. For each, list: current APY, smart contract risk rating, liquidity depth, and whether the yield is real or inflationary. Recommend the best risk-adjusted allocation."

---

### Prompt 9 — Macro → Crypto Playbook
> "Given today's macro conditions: [paste: Fed rate decision / CPI data / DXY level / equity market move]. Build a 2-week crypto trading playbook. Which assets benefit most from these conditions? What narratives get a tailwind? What correlation plays make sense (risk-on/risk-off)? Give 3 specific trade ideas."

---

### Prompt 10 — Exit Strategy Architect
> "I'm holding [token] at an average entry of [price], currently at [current price], up [X]%. Help me build a tiered exit strategy: (1) a conservative exit plan protecting most gains, (2) an aggressive hold-for-more plan with defined stop triggers, (3) a hedge strategy using options or short perps. Include specific price targets and rationale for each tier."
